Announcement

We are thrilled to announce the launch of the Strength-Based Assessment Lab

At the heart of the Strength-Based Assessment Lab lies a profound mission — one that puts every child's gifts, talents, and potential at the centre of their educational experience.

  • To uncover and identify the strengths of those who are struggling in their current educational settings.
  • To empower students, families, educators, and professionals to create positive, effective, and collaborative learning experiences.
  • To foster a world where every student feels valued and is empowered to demonstrate their abilities and skills in a personal and meaningful manner.

Our pioneering approach utilizes the meticulously developed Suite of Tools™ (SOT), a field-tested methodology that identifies and nurtures strengths, interests, and talents.

Our Process

The Suite of Tools™ Protocol

A purposefully designed four-tool protocol to elicit information about student interests, moments of personal best, challenges, goals, and learning preferences — all in an innovative and enjoyable process.

1

Intake & Matching

After an intake interview, you are matched with a highly qualified case manager who will work with your learner and family, using an innovative and enjoyable process to identify your child's strengths and talents — bringing in school and community members as appropriate.

2

Collaborative Findings

The case manager collates findings collected during the process and shares them in a collaborative meeting. Each ally participates in creating opportunities that will enrich and support your child's unique strengths, helping them develop, take root, and thrive.

3

Detailed Report & Plan

The Case Manager documents all findings and suggestions in a detailed report with recommendations for implementation. At a final meeting, you create a talent development plan designed to support your child's strengths — for a lifetime.

"Your family is left with a full heart, a fresh perspective, and an energized understanding of your unique child that will ripple out positively through their lifetime."
